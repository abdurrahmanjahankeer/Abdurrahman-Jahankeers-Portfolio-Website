Project 4: Video Presentation on Computers

The project is a video presentation on the history of computers, from their origin as human "computers" and mechanical machinery to modern digital computers. The presentation highlights key milestones such as Babbage’s plans, ENIAC, IBM mainframes, personal computers, and the birth of the internet. The presentation shows how computers developed from simple calculators to essential tools in shaping today’s digital world.

Project Partners: Abdurrahmaan Shaikh & Dawood Khalid.
